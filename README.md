# Torch Slabs Renewed

A maintained, updated port of the original [Torch Slabs Mod](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/torchslabs-mod) for **Minecraft 1.20.1+ (Forge)**.

Vanilla torches, lanterns, candles and more were never meant to sit on slabs, hang under ledges or rest on lily pads — until now. This mod lets you place your existing vanilla light items in dozens of spots the base game won't allow, with no new items to craft: just right-click with the vanilla item.

## ✨ What you can do

**Torches & Soul Torches**
- Stand them on **top of bottom slabs** and **stairs**.
- **Hang them under the underside of slabs** (both top and bottom slabs), pointing down like a ceiling torch. *(New!)*
- Place them on the **sides of slabs and stairs**, snapping to the top or bottom half depending on where you're aiming.
- Place them on **lily pads**.

**Lanterns & Soul Lanterns**
- Sit them on **top of bottom slabs and stairs**, or **hang them under top slabs/stairs**.
- Place them on the **sides of solid blocks, slabs, stairs, fences and walls**. *(Walls are new!)*
- On block sides they snap to the **upper or lower half** based on where you look.
- Place them on **lily pads**.

**Candles (all 17 dye colors)**
- Place them on **top of bottom slabs, stairs and lily pads**, and stack up to 4 like vanilla candles.

**End Rods**
- Place them on **slabs**, standing up or hanging down, and stack them into pillars.

**Chains**
- Run them **on top of, under and through slabs**, hanging and linking together. *(Now enabled!)*

## ⚙️ Configuration

A server config (`torchslabs-server.toml`) lets you fine-tune everything:

- **Toggle lanterns on block sides** and on the **lower half** of blocks.
- **Toggle torches on the upper half** of wall blocks.
- **Disable hanging torches under slabs/stairs** entirely. *(New!)*
- **Interactive-block list** — blocks (crafting table, furnace, doors, shulker boxes, etc.) that require sneak-clicking so you don't open their GUI by accident. Fully customizable.

## 🔗 Mod compatibility

- **Any modded slabs, stairs, walls and fences** work automatically, as long as they extend vanilla `SlabBlock` / `StairBlock` / `WallBlock` / `FenceBlock`.
- **[Amendments](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/amendments)** — Torch Slabs steps aside for lanterns so Amendments' lantern physics keep working. Toggleable in the config.
- **[Supplementaries](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/supplementaries)** — Torch Slabs steps aside for lanterns; Supplementaries' own lights (candle holders, sconces) already place on and under slabs natively. Toggleable in the config.

## 📌 Notes

- **Soul Torches and Soul Lanterns** share every ability with their normal counterparts.
- This build targets **Forge 1.20.1+**. For older versions, use the original mod.
- No new items or recipes — everything uses the vanilla items you already have.
